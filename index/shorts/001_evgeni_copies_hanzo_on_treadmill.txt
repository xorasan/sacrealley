====== 001 evgeni copies hanzo on treadmill ======
Created Saturday 24 June 2023


Evgeni is working out in the dark in the gym. He's running at a steady pace on a treadmill.
He's serious about his workouts. The gym is dimly lit, enough to show his features.

The sound of his feet & the noise of the treadmill are about the only sounds in the gym.

All of a sudden, the room lights up, enough to grab Evgeni's attention.
As the camera pans, the door is sliding open, at its very end.

Hanzo appears standing in the middle of it.
His gym bag hanging by his fingers behind his back.
